and give my opinion and prediction for their potential role in our team. 
All files are private and not accessible by anyone but people who have link address 

Methods 

All of the reports are made watching live games or/and games and clips from Synergy. Used stats as a secondary tool which can give me better picture about particular player and track his Improvements in some areas. Most of the players have typical playing style "DNA" and could be described in most of the cases.

Video Clips

Video clips are made by analizing player typical skill(s) or typical move(s). Same pricipals I used to describe player weaknesses and mistakes he repeat most often. Try not to glorify or "kill" a player puting together selective clips about best or worst moments which can bias opinion about him.

Feel

 Feel, intuition, imagination.. I know, those words not exist in world of math/stat science, including analytic in sports. In my case I use feel as a part of folowing skills: experience from the court during playing carrer, basketball knowledge I "collected" from coaches I had or I met later, analyzing players character, reading about the game
